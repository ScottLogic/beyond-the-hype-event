About the event

AI is changing everything about the way we approach software engineering and product development. The technology has reached an inflexion point in capability — and as a result, we need to shift our focus from merely augmenting developers with AI tools, to creating the environment that allows AI to be truly successful.

We need to reshape our processes — moving away from ones designed around human strengths and weaknesses, towards new ones shaped around the strengths and weaknesses of AI. This is not going to be easy, and there are a great many important questions we don't yet have answers to.

Join us for an evening of talks from practitioners and conversations with your peers — to share experiences and insights, learn together, and start forming a view of what our future looks like.
