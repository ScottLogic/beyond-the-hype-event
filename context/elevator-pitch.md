This document is a brief elevator pitch for the event
===
AI is changing everything about the way we approach software engineering and product development. Late last year the technology reached an inflexion point in capability, and as a result, we need to shift our focus from augmenting developers with AI tools, to creating the environment that allows AI to be successful. We need to move reshape our processes from ones designed around human strengths and weaknesses, to new ones shaped around the strengths and weaknesses of AI. 

This is not going to be easy, and there are a great many important questions that we don’t have answers to yet. 

We want to come together and start tackling these hard questions, to share experiences and insights, learn and converse. And together start forming a view of what our future looks like. 

Join us for an evening of talks from practitioners, and conversations with your peers.
