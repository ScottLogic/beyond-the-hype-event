The moment everything changes

AI is no longer a future consideration — it is reshaping software engineering right now. From code generation to autonomous agents, the tools available to engineering teams have transformed dramatically. The question is no longer whether AI will change how we build software, but how teams choose to adapt, lead, and stay in control of the process.

The risks are real. Over-reliance on AI-generated code can introduce subtle bugs and erode the deep understanding that makes great software teams effective. Security posture, code quality, and architectural integrity all require human judgement — informed, sharpened, and directed by engineers who understand both the opportunity and the danger.

This event brings together practitioners who are navigating that tension every day. Expect honest conversations about what is working, what is not, and what it means to build software responsibly in the AI age. No hype. No easy answers. Just the hard-won insight of people doing the work.
